Goodbyes Lyrics

Publish date: 2024-07-07

“Goodbyes” is a song by American rapper and singer Post Malone, featuring vocals from American rapper Young Thug.

It was released as the second single from Post Malone’s third studio album, Hollywood’s Bleeding, on July 5, 2019.

Goodbyes Lyrics is about the difficulties of ending a toxic relationship and the mixed feelings that come with it.

The music video, directed by Colin Tilley, depicts Post Malone as a zombie who gets stabbed and resurrected.

The song peaked at number three on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and received positive reviews from critics.
