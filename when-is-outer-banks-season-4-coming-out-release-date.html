When Is Outer Banks Season 4 Coming Out: Release Date

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Netflix officially renewed Outer Banks for a fourth season ahead of the premiere of season 3 on February 23, 2023. The renewal announcement came as no surprise, considering the show’s immense popularity and positive reception from audiences. According to a production list by ProductionWeekly, Outer Banks, season 4 was scheduled to begin filming on May 30th, 2023, and then run through the majority of 2023 with an estimated wrap date of December 15th, 2023.

While there is no confirmed release date for Outer Banks Season 4 at the moment, fans can take solace in the fact that production is underway. Following the renewal announcement, the cast and crew began preparations for the upcoming season.

Filming schedules, scriptwriting, and other production aspects are being meticulously planned to ensure the high-quality standards set by the previous seasons. In this article, we will explore the current status of the show and speculate on the possible release date for the highly anticipated fourth season.

When Does the Next Season of Outer Banks Come Out?

Outer Banks might only have released its third season on February 23, 2023, but it was already renewed for a fourth season ahead of the season 3 release. At an immersive fan event called Poguelandia in Huntington Beach, California, the case, including Madelyn Cline and Chase Stokes took to the stage to surprise fans with the announcement. The event held on February 18, was a celebration that also featured performances by Khalid, Lil Baby, alt-J, and Elley Duhé.

The co-creators, showrunners, and executive producers of Outer-Banks, Jonas Pate, Josh Pate, and Shannon Burke were confirmed to continue in their respective roles for Season 4. In their interview, they expressed their excitement for future seasons, hinting at more high-stakes adventures and thrilling plot twists. The dedication and passion of the show’s creators, combined with the fervent fanbase, make the prospect of Outer Banks Season 4 all the more promising.

Initially scheduled to begin filming on May 30th, 2023, and wrap up by December 15th, 2023, the production of Outer Banks Season 4 faced delays due to the Writers Guild of America (WGA) strike. However, an updated report from ProductionWeekly reveals that filming is set to commence on June 12, 2023, with an anticipated completion date of December 15th, 2023. These production dates provide a glimpse into the show’s progress and commitment to delivering another captivating season.

While an official release date for Outer Banks Season 4 has yet to be released by Netflix, we can make predictions based on previous release patterns. Outer Banks Season 3 concluded with its final episode titled “The Holy Grail,” which aired on July 30, 2021. It left viewers with a dramatic cliffhanger, raising numerous questions and setting the stage for further adventures.

Speculations on Outer Banks Season 4 Release Date

In the past, there have been varying time gaps between the release of consecutive seasons of Outer Banks. The time difference between Season 1 and Season 2 of Outer Banks was approximately 14 months. Season 1 premiered in April 2020, and Season 2 was released in July 2021. This long gap was partially due to production delays caused by the COVID-19 pandemic. With Season 3 of Outer Banks premiering at the end of February 2023, it’s plausible to expect the release of Season 4 in the summer or fall of 2024 should its production wrap up by the end of the year.

However, it is important to acknowledge that unforeseen circumstances, production schedules and factors such as the writer’s strike can impact the release timeline. Nevertheless, the fact that Season 4 was renewed well ahead of Season 3’s premiere indicates that fans may not have to wait as long as they did for the previous season.

As fans eagerly anticipate the arrival of Season 4, the creators have exciting news to share with the fanbase. In an interview, they revealed that they are no longer adhering to their original plan of ending the series after four or five seasons. They even playfully mentioned the possibility of a potential 17th season set on Mars, hinting at their willingness to explore open territories. The creator’s openness to extending the series beyond their initial plan adds an intriguing element to the future of Outer Banks.
