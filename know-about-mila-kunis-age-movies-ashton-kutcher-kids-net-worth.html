Know About Mila Kunis; Age, Movies, Ashton Kutcher, Kids, Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-09-13

After dating for three years, Mila Kunis finally tied the wedding knot with her husband, who was the co-star on the TV show. Well, the couple has been relishing their married life as a parents of two children.

Mila Kunis is a Ukrainian-American actress known for her role as Jackie Burkhart in American sit-com, That ‘70s Show. She recently appeared as Audrey Stockton in the 2018 action spy spoof comedy movie, The Spy Who Dumped Me.

Mila Kunis Bio; Family, Education, Height

Milena Markovna Kunis was born on 14 August 1983 as a daughter of Mark Kunis and Elvira Kunis in Chernivtsi, Ukrainian SSR. Her father is a mechanical engineer and her mother is a physics teacher who runs a pharmacy. She has a brother named Michael Kunis. Her family moved to the United States when she was seven years old.

Kunis with a height of 5 feet 4 inches (1.63 meters), attended Fairfax Senior High School in Los Angeles. Later, she briefly attended UCLA and Loyola Marymount University.

Her Career; Movies, Net Worth

Mila made her acting breakthrough from the 1994 soap drama, Days of Our Lives. She then appeared in the 1994 horror movie Piranha. Moreover, she was cast Rachael Newman in the 2002 horror movie, American Psycho 2.

Some of her movies work include Max Payne (2008), Extract (2009), Friends With Benefits (2011), Jupiter Ascending (2015), and The Spy Who Dumped Me (2018). Meanwhile, Kunis appeared as Jackie Burkhardt in the American sitcom, That ’70s Show from 2006 to 2019.

Well, Mila is one of the highest earning actresses in Hollywood. She became the highest paid actress of 2017 with $15.5 million. As of now, her net worth reaches an estimated $65 million.

Married Actor Husband; Kids

The 35-year-old is currently married to actor husband Ashton Kutcher from the past three years. The couple got hitched on 4 July 2015 in Oak Glen, California after dating for three years. Ashton, aged 41 is an actor, who starred alongside Mila on That ’70s Show.

Mila and her husband are the parents of two kids from their relationship. Firstly, they welcomed a daughter named Wyatt Isabelle Kutcher on 1st October 2014. Later, she gave birth to a son, Dimitri Portwood Kutcher on 30 November 2016.

Before Ashton, she was in long term relationship with actor Macaulay Culkin. They started dating in 2002 but ended their relationship in 2011.
