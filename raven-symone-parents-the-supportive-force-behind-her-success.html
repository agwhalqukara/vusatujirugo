Raven Symone Parents: The Supportive Force Behind Her Success

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Raven Symone is a familiar face to most people. She portrayed Olivia Kendall, the adorable little girl on The Cosby Show, and she also took on the role of Nicole Lee in Hangin’ with Mr. Cooper.

Additionally, she was the main character in the show titled That’s So Raven.

Throughout her career, this multi-talented artist collaborated closely with other high-profile Hollywood celebrities from a young age.

She established herself as a household name in the entertainment industry. However, have you ever wondered about her parents?

Who are Raven Symone Parents?

American couple Christopher Pearman and Lydia Pearman are Raven Symone’s parents, and they have played a crucial role in supporting her career.

Their unwavering support has been instrumental in her success, and she maintains a strong and close relationship with both of them.

Raven Symone Biography

Raven-Symoné Christina Pearman was born to Lydia and Christopher Pearman in Atlanta, Georgia, United States of America, on December 10, 1985.

She is currently 37 years old. Raven Symone is an American citizen, and her Zodiac sign is Sagittarius. She has a younger brother named Blaize Pearman.

As an infant, she began her career at Young Faces Inc. Modeling Agency in Atlanta, appearing in local print advertisements.

At the age of two, she joined New York City-based Ford Models, featuring in ads for Cool Whip, Jell-O, Fisher-Price, and Ritz crackers.

When Raven Symone was three years old, her parents relocated to Ossining, New York.

Career

Raven is a versatile entertainer, encompassing roles as an actress, songwriter, singer, rapper, producer, and dancer.

Her acting journey commenced in 1989 when she first entered the industry.

Initially, she auditioned for a part in Bill Cosby’s Ghost Dad; however, she was considered too young for the role.

Nevertheless, her talent caught the eye of Bill Cosby himself.

He was so impressed that he offered her a role on The Bill Cosby Show in 1989, where she portrayed his step-granddaughter named Olivia.

Though Ghost Dad didn’t work out, her career took off, and she has since graced both the big screen and various television shows.

Raven also embarked on her singing career at the age of four, officially launching it when she was just seven years old.

Raven Symone Parents and Net Worth

Raven’s estimated net worth stands at approximately $40 million. The American actress and singer achieved fame during her childhood while starring in The Cosby Show.

Her acting career, coupled with lucrative licensing deals with Disney, contributed to her amassing millions of dollars.

In addition to her financial success, Raven owns a 4,000-square-foot residence in Sherman Oaks, California, which had a value of $2.1 million as of 2020.
