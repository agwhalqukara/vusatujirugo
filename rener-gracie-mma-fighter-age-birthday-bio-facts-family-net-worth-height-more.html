Rener Gracie is a Brazilian-American jiu-jitsu practitioner, a lecturer at Gracie University, and a co-creator of the Gracie University online program. A member of the Gracie family, he is the grandson of Grandmaster Hélio Gracie and the eldest son of Grandmaster Rorion Gracie, who was one of the first members of the family to bring Gracie jiu-jitsu to the United States with Carley. Gracie and Carlson Gracie. Rener is the husband of actress, model and former professional wrestler Eve Torres. Rener spent over 25 years at Gracie Academy studying with Rorion and Helio Gracie. Rener and his brother Ryron Gracie founded Gracie University, an online martial arts learning center, and developed distance learning packages for their exclusive courses. He is also the founder and CEO of Quikflip Apparel, a company that specializes in backpacks.
