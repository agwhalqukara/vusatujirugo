Kal Penn: Harold and Kumar Actor Steps Out the Closet, Proudly Gay and Engaged

Publish date: 2024-09-15
  • Kal Penn publicly disclosed he is gay and noted it is something known by many of those close to him
  • The actor noted he is delighted to have Indian parents that understand him and show support for his career
  • The thespian revealed that he is the extrovert in the family as his parents, lover and brother are shy and quiet

Actor Kal Penn has, for the first time, publicly opened up on his sexuality and relationship with his fiancé.

According to HuffPost, the thespian disclosed in an interview on Sunday, October 31, that he has been engaged to his partner, Josh, for over 11 years.

The Indian-American actor, famous for his role in Harold and Kumar, said he discovered he was gay a bit late in life compared to others.

Speaking exclusively to People, Penn said he was glad to discover his sexuality, noting there was no timeline for such stuff.

He expressed his delight at having Indian parents who support his dreams and being very welcoming.

Always been an open book

In his new book, You Can't Be Serious, which he is currently promoting, he noted being open with people he interacts with no matter where they meet.

He said despite his bubbly self - his fiancé, parents and brother, are fairly quiet and do not love attention.

Penn said he and Josh were in the planning stages for their wedding but disagreed on its size.

While he wants a big one, Josh is asking for just a small event with family and close friends - adding they might as well meet in the middle.

Kim Kardashian spotted with Pete Davidson

In other international news, reality TV star Kim Kardashian was spotted hanging out with comedian Pete Davidson over the weekend.

According to People, the 41-year-old joined her sister Kourtney Kardashian and her lover, Travis Barker, for the fun-filled night.

They were spotted enjoying their time on a rollercoaster at Knott's Scary Farm in Buena Park, California, on Friday, October 29, as they kicked off their Halloween.

They also had friends around, and during the ride, Kim and Pete were spotted holding hands together, which left some of their fans puzzled.

However, a source who spoke to People noted that the 27-year-old comedian hangs in the same circle as Kim, so they are bound to spend more time together.
