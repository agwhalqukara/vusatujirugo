Pauline Sinclair: Facts about Vin Diesel Daughter

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Pauline Sinclair is the youngest kid of Hollywood star Vin Diesel and the Mexican model Paloma Jiménez. Moreover, she is in the public eye just because of her name and as a celebrity kid. Do you want to know why? At the end of this writing, you will learn everything about her name and her life. Here are the facts that might interest you.

Who is Pauline Sinclair? Age and Early Life

Pauline is the daughter of international icon Vin Diesel and Mexican model Paloma Jiménez. She was born on March 16, 2015. Her name was designated in honor of late actor Paul Walker, the dearest friend to Vin Diesel, who died in a severe accident. Vin often regards her as Little Angel.

Unlike her siblings, she is in the limelight and loves the public eye. The star kid has a mixed ethnicity as Diesel is Caucasian and Paloma is from Mexico. She has two siblings; Hania and Vincent Sinclair.

Pauline Sinclair’s Parents Relationship

Pauline’s parents are in a relationship since 2007 but haven’t married to this date. Paloma didn’t regard Vin in the beginning, but later she got attentive towards him as he did everything possible to impress her. Despite having a huge age gap of 16 years, no obstacles affect their relationship.

To this date, they are in a prosperous relationship. They share their sorrow and joy; Paloma joins him in film premieres and walks the red carpet when Vin receives the Hollywood Walk of Fame Award.

Father Vin Diesel

Born as Mark Sinclair on July 18, 1967, her father is a very famous personality. He grew up in California with Paul. Paul is Vin’s beloved twin brother. His stepfather and his mother brought him up. His stepfather used to manage theatre and demonstrate acting to his students at Brooklyn, and there, Vin began to act in plays from an early age of 8 years. He joined Hunter College in New York to learn to screenwriting.

Before he began his career, he became a bouncer in the nightclub, spent extra time in screenwriting, and wrote his first screenplay, Doormen, in 1997. He began his acting career officially from the 1998 film “Saving Private Ryan.” In 2000, he got featured in “Pitch Black” as a lead role.

After that, he got featured as the main actor in many blockbusters like the “Fast and Furious” series, “XXX: The Return of Xander Cage,” “A Man Apart,” “The Pacifier,” “The Last Witch Hunter” and many more. In 2002, he founded Tigon Studios and had also been working as a movie producer since then.

He had directed movies such as “The Pacifier,” “Iron Giant,” “Guardians of the Galaxy,” etc. Currently, he is working on the movie F9. After years of struggle and hardwork, today, he is one of the most successful and highly paid Hollywood actors. Most of the fans even knew him by Dominic Toretto, a character of the Fast and the Furious series. Vin is living in an 11000 square feet mansion in Atlanta, and his total worth is massive 225 million dollars. 

Mother Paloma Jiménez

Pauline’s mother is also a known name to the world. Originally from Guerro, she was born on August 22, 1983. The information of her family and siblings is unknown. It is believed to have mixed ethnicity and is often called by her other name Karla Jiminez. Since her early life, she entered the modeling field as her beautiful body features also supported her.

She has black hair with black eyes. The beautiful lady also has a perfect height of 5 feet 10 inches, best for a model like her. Paloma’s body measurement is 33-24-35 inches respectively. As a model, her fame as a fashion designer goes far and wide. “Mexican Maxim Magazine,” a renowned fashion magazine, has also promoted her modeling career.

In 2006, she also appeared in the fashion exhibition “Ready to Wear.” She then migrated to the USA from Mexico in search of better opportunities. Today, Paloma is famous for her collaboration with big companies like Coca-cola, Pantene, and Honda. She has contributed to modeling centers like ‘ID Model Management’ and ‘Look Models.’ She has also appeared on the TV show “Otro Rollo.” Her total wealth’s about 5 million dollars. 

Pauline Sinclair Siblings: Hania Riley Sinclair and Vincent Sinclair

Pauline has two elder siblings: Hania Riley Sinclair and Vincent Sinclair. Hania is the eldest daughter of Vin and Paloma, born in April of 2008, while Vin was acting in the film “Fast and Furious 4.” Her family members often called her Simile. Being Vin’s first child, he often calls her Alpha Angel. She supports and follows her parents in events like a movie premiere. She is now living together with her parents in Los Angeles. Along with her dad, she has featured in “Fast and Furious: Spy Racers.”

Vincent Sinclair, named after his father’s stage name, was born in 2010. Diesel sometimes posts his son mimicking his film character on Instagram. Vin and Vincent also made a video regarding COVID-19, which was quite popular on Instagram. Vin loves his children very much. On his Instagram, with 71.2 million followers, he always posts images and videos with his children.
