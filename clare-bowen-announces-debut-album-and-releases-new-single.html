Clare Bowen Announces Debut Album and Releases New Single

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Clare Bowen is best known for playing Scarlett O’Connor in the hit US drama Nashville. But with the series having come to an end (three more episodes to go in the UK) she is ready to take on the music industry with her own musical output.

This August she will release her long awaited self-titled debut album and today she released her brand new single ‘Let It Rain‘. The track is already a huge fan favourite thanks to the steller live performances Bowen delivered of the hypnotic track during the Nashville Farewell UK Tour earlier this year.

The Nashville based Australian singer has a unique vocal tone that has a haunting ability to draw in listeners and she is one of the most exciting country acts currently on the scene. The full tracklist for her debut record is as follows:

Let It Rain
Doors & Corridors
Tide Rolls In
Aves’ Song

All The Beds I’ve Made
Lullabye
Sweet William
Lijah & The Shadow
Little by Little
Grace Of God & You
Warrior

Its August 31st release date via BMG records is one of the most anticipated of the year and British fans will be able to catch Clare Bowen live this year as she brings her first headlining tour to the UK this September. Tickets for the shows are on sale now!

Make sure you follow Clare Bowen on Twitter @clarembee to keep up to date with all their latest music and tour news!
